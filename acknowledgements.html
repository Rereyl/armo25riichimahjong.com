Australian Riichi Mahjong Association

The Australian Riichi Mahjong Association is Australia's national representative organisation for riichi mahjong. Their goals are to promote the sport of riichi mahjong in Australia, create networks between players, and to represent Australia on the world stage.

Brisbane Mahjong

Brisbane Mahjong welcomes all players of both Riichi and Hong Kong mahjong to come and enjoy a variety of new ways to play the game and have fun. There are both casual and competitive options available to suit everyone's preferred play.

Brisbane Riichi Mahjong League

We are the Brisbane Riichi Mahjong League (BRML), a community dedicated to playing and promoting Riichi Mahjong.
