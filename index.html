Welcome to the Australian Riichi Mahjong Open

Proudly hosted by the Australian Riichi Mahjong Association and held in Brisbane, Australia, ARMO2025 will be the fourth iteration of Australia's premier national riichi mahjong tournament and welcomes all players to compete. Come join us on Sat 29th and Sun 30th of November, 2025 for a two day tournment where you will have the opportunity to face off against players around the nation.

Acknowledgements

ARMO25 is brought to you by ARMA, Southside Brisbane Mahjong Social and Brisbane Riichi Mahjong League.
