Tournament Format



The tournament will consist of nine, 80 minute hanchan in a round robin format with no cutoff. Tables that exceed 80 minutes will finish their current hand and play a final hand, if applicable, then proceed to record their scores and summon a referee to check and sign the scoresheet before leaving the table.
