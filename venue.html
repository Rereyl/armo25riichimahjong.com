Brisbane and Four Points by Sheraton

Brisbane is a relaxed city that blends a growing cultural scene with plenty of outdoor charm. Set along the Brisbane River, it offers a mix of modern city life and natural beauty, with highlights like South Bankâ€™s riverside parks, galleries, and casual dining spots.

ARMO 2025 will be held at Four Points by Sheraton Hotel located in the heart of the Brisbane CBD with ease of access to all facilities and food that Brisbane has to offer.

Most public transport systems (not including Airtrain) in Brisbane and the surrounding area have a flat rate of 50c per trip. You can pay for fares with your phone, debit/credit card, paper tickets or by using a Go card which can be purchased at any newsagency. It is recommended to use a navigation service like Google Maps to provide accurate directions.

Train

You can take any train line to Central Station with a 12 minute walk to the venue.

Bus

When travelling from outside the CBD, take a bus to King George Square then a 7 min walk to the venue. It is generally faster to walk if you are already in the CBD.

CityCat (Ferry)

Disembark from North Quay terminal then walk 15 minutes to the venue.

Car

There is limited paid street and parking complex parking which can be found on Mary Street and surrounding streets.
